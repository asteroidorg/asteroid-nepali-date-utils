## [0.0.1] - 2026-06-01

### Added

- Initial public release of `@asteroidstudio/date-utils`.
- AD and BS date conversion helpers.
- `NepaliFunctions` namespace with AD and BS utility groups.
- Date parsing, formatting, and comparison helpers.
- Unicode conversion helpers for Nepali numerals.
- Number-to-words helpers in English and Nepali.
- Convenience helpers for creating dates and finding month lengths.
